🔄 Depop Listing Refresher

About

This extension automatically refreshes your Depop listings by updating them, which pushes them to the top of search results.

How to Use
  1. Go to your Depop selling page
  2. Look for the draggable control panel in the bottom-right corner
  3. Click "Start Refreshing" to begin
  4. Wait for it to complete
💡 The extension refreshes listings by updating them via the API - no deletion involved!
Extension is active. Visit your selling page to use it.
